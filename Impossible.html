Impossible List

I first heard about an Impossible List by a Youtuber/Productivity expert named Thomas Frank and he got it from someone else. This particular page is actually inspired by the one Thomas made. Now how is an impossible list different form a bucket it, well an I.L is one that would evolve once an expierence is met and just builds upon the original goal. For example if you had a goal to bench 200lbs then maybe after that a new goal could be to bench 250lbs. Also there is no particular time limit to accomplish that goal.
